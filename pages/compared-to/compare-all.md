      ## How fees are calculated

      Each platform card shows the amount you keep after all fees are deducted
      from your total ticket sales for a year. Fees include annual subscription
      costs, per-ticket platform fees, and payment processing charges.

      The estimate assumes one ticket per payment transaction. A booking with
      several tickets usually incurs a fixed payment-processing charge once,
      so its real cost may be lower. The calculator cannot represent every
      minimum fee, cap, VAT rule, pricing tier, negotiated rate or optional
      buyer-paid charge. The comparison pages linked from the cards list fee
      sources and review dates where available.

      Platforms are sorted by total cost, with the lowest fees first. Chobble Tickets
      is highlighted in purple so you can spot it quickly.

      Click **Compare** on any platform to see a detailed side-by-side breakdown.

      You can also find Chobble Tickets on

      to see how it compares with other ticketing software.
