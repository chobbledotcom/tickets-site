      ## Payment processing costs

      Ticket payments go straight to the organiser's own Stripe, Square or SumUp account. Chobble does not hold ticket income or deduct a platform fee.

      The selected payment provider charges its normal transaction fee. That fee is separate from Chobble's hosting price and varies by provider, card type and country.

      | Cost | Managed hosting | Self-hosting |
      |---|---:|---:|
      | Chobble Tickets | £50/year or £5/month | £0 |
      | Per-ticket platform fee | £0 | £0 |
      | Payment provider fee | Provider's normal rate | Provider's normal rate |
      | Events and tickets | No application limit | No application limit |
      | Product features | All included | All included |

      Chobble Tickets uses one currency and one active card payment provider per site. Free events can run without a payment provider.
