      # Class and course booking software

      Take bookings for recurring classes and multi-week courses, with a capacity for each session and a deadline before each one starts. For one-off sessions, see the [workshops page](/perfect-for/workshops/).
  - type: split-icon-links

      ## Recurring sessions with per-session capacity

      [Daily events](/features/daily-events/) run a class on the days you
      choose, with a separate capacity for each session. Students pick
      their session from a calendar, and you can block out half term and
      bank holidays with holiday dates.

      [Registration deadlines](/features/registration-deadlines/) close
      bookings before each session starts, so you have time to prepare.
      [Custom questions](/features/custom-questions/) collect details at
      checkout, such as experience level or allergies, and the answers
      appear across the admin interface.

      Walk-ins can be added as
      [manual attendees](/features/manual-attendees/) from the admin panel.
      When a new term starts,
      [duplicate events](/features/duplicate-events/) clone last term's
      setup without re-entering the details.

      ## Courses, materials, and class lists

      For multi-week courses,
      [parent/child listings](/features/parent-child-listings/) can require
      a materials kit or other extra alongside the course booking, so no
      one arrives without one. For expensive courses,
      [deposits](/features/deposits-and-balance-payments/) take part of the
      fee at booking and send a link to pay the balance later.

      [Bulk email](/features/bulk-email/) sends a message to everyone
      booked on a class, with previews, drafts, and an unsubscribe link.
      Attendee lists export as [CSV](/features/csv-export/) for registers
      and planning.

      Chobble Tickets charges a flat £50/year or £5/month with no
      per-booking fees, and payments go directly to your own Stripe,
      Square, or SumUp account. Charities and community groups get a 50%
      discount on the annual price.
