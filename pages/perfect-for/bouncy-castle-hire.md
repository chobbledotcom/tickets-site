      # Bouncy castle hire booking software

      Take inflatable hire bookings online with per-day pricing, delivery and collection run sheets, and deposits. Managed hosting is £50/year or £5/month, with no commission on bookings.
  - type: split-icon-links
    content: |
      ## Bookings, deliveries, and collections

      Each inflatable is a [daily listing](/features/daily-events/) with its
      own capacity per date, so a castle cannot be booked twice for the same
      day. Customers pick their date from a calendar, and
      [multi-day hire](/features/multi-day-hire/) lets them choose how long
      to keep the unit, with a separate price for each day of the hire.

      Delivery and collection are handled by the built-in
      [logistics system](/features/logistics/). Each booking gets a drop-off
      agent and a collection agent, and drivers log in to see a run sheet of
      their deliveries and mark each leg done.

      Payments go through Stripe, Square, or SumUp directly to your own
      account. Chobble Tickets takes no percentage of the booking.

      ## Inspections, deposits, and paperwork

      When a unit goes out for repair or its annual PIPA inspection, a
      [servicing event](/features/servicing-events/) blocks it out of
      availability without creating a customer booking. The cost of the
      inspection can be recorded against the servicing event in the
      [ledger](/features/ledger/).

      [Deposits](/features/deposits-and-balance-payments/) let you take part
      of the price at booking and send the customer a link to pay the
      balance later. [Packages](/features/packages/) bundle a castle with
      extras, such as a generator or soft play, into one booking with one
      price.

      Customers agree to your
      [terms and conditions](/features/terms-and-conditions/) before they
      can book. [Custom questions](/features/custom-questions/) collect the
      details you need at checkout, such as venue surface, access width, and
      whether mains power is available.
