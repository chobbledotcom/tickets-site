      ## Tickets, subs, and session bookings

      Match and fixture tickets work like any other event. Each attendee
      gets a QR code ticket by email, and stewards
      [scan it at the gate](/features/qr-code-check-ins/) with a phone
      camera.

      [Purchasable events](/features/purchasable-events/) collect payments
      that have no event to attend, such as annual membership dues or
      seasonal subs. Each payment is a one-off; Chobble Tickets does not
      set up recurring billing or direct debits, so members pay again when
      the next period starts.

      For training sessions, [daily events](/features/daily-events/) give
      each date its own capacity and a calendar picker, with blackout
      dates for weeks when training is off.

      ## Built for committees

      [Groups](/features/groups/) organise a season of fixtures together,
      and attendees can book several fixtures in a single checkout.
      [Promo codes](/features/promo-codes-and-add-ons/) give members a
      discount code to use at checkout.

      Payments go through Stripe, Square, or SumUp directly to the club's
      own account. Community groups get a 50% discount, which brings the
      annual price to £25.

      Every income figure comes from a
      [double-entry ledger](/features/ledger/), with date and event
      filters and per-account statements, and any list can be exported as
      a [CSV file](/features/csv-export/) for the treasurer.
